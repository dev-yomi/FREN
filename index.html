Frequently Asked Questions

1. Wat iz dis contract about, fren?

Dis contract iz for a token named FREN. Frens can stake FREN tokens in pairs! Bein frens! Dey earn interest, but dey gotta hab at least a certain amount of time as a fren pair.

2. Why, fren?

Dev fren doesnt like dat most memecoins are scarin away da ppl! Too many non-frens trying to hurt da frens wiv badness. FREN is a fun way for frens to be frens. Jus like crypto shud be.

3. How do I gib my FREN tokens for staking, fren?

You use da "stake" button wid da amount of FREN tokens you want to stake. Dis checks if you hab enough tokens, den it burns your tokens. If dere are no other frens in da queue, you will wait 4 a fren. If dere iz another fren, you two become a frenpair.

4. Can I stop being a fren, fren?

Yes, fren. You use da "Stop Being Fren" button. You'll get ur stake back n any rewards you earned. Some ov ur rewards iz shared with da other FREN holders!

5. Can I leave da queue, fren?

Yes, fren. You use da "Leave Queue" button if you been alone for a while! You'll get ur stake back, plus sum free interest as a sorry for u waitin! Dis interest is only earned if nobody becomes ur fren b4 u leave.

6. How does da burn work, fren?

Da burn rate changes according to da total supply of FREN. It increases or decreases by a little bit for every 0.1% change in supply.

7. What iz dynamic reflections?

Some of da burned tokens r put in a big pot for all da frens! Dis is automatically applied to your balance, it iz proportional to ur share of tokens, so it can go down if you don't make more FRENs!

7. What iz daily interest bonus?

For every day dat u n a fren are in a pair, you will earn 0.01% more interest! Dis is compounded, so da longer you stay frens - da more FREN you get! Da bonus iz reset when you stop being frens.

8. Iz FREN valuable?

Surely any1 wants as many FRENs as possible? Da value in a FREN is de value you gib dat fren! FRENs!*
